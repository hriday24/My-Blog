must-visit places

Kidzania, Mumbai, India

I visited Kidzania, Mumbai in December, 2019.

It is a fun place with activities of all types. Cooking, flying an airplane, driving a car, making doughnuts are just some of the 200+ activities.

Boss Baby  
Click the image to watch

A fun film to watch with friends and family.   

The Boss Baby is a really fun film for the whole family. The baby is super cute, and everyone will fall in love with the baby in an instant. The sibling rivalry plot is convincing, then the fantasy kicks in. The jokes are funny and family friendly.
